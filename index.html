Breakfast Recipes

Avocado Toast

Ingredients: Whole grain bread, Avocado, Lemon juice, Salt, Pepper

Instructions: Toast the bread. Mash the avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Spread on the toast.

Greek Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients: Greek yogurt, Honey, Mixed berries, Granola

Instructions: Layer yogurt, honey, berries, and granola in a glass.

Lunch Recipes

Quinoa Salad

Ingredients: Quinoa, Cherry tomatoes, Cucumber, Feta cheese, Olive oil, Lemon juice

Instructions: Cook quinoa. Mix with chopped vegetables, feta, olive oil, and lemon juice.

Chicken Wrap

Ingredients: Whole wheat wrap, Chicken breast, Lettuce, Tomato, Avocado

Instructions: Grill chicken and slice. Wrap with lettuce, tomato, and avocado.

Dinner Recipes

Grilled Salmon

Ingredients: Salmon fillets, Olive oil, Lemon, Dill, Salt, Pepper

Instructions: Brush salmon with olive oil, lemon, dill, salt, and pepper. Grill until cooked through.

Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients: Mixed vegetables, Soy sauce, Garlic, Ginger, Olive oil

Instructions: Stir-fry vegetables with garlic and ginger in olive oil. Add soy sauce to taste.
